# Churn-Trends-and-Key-Drivers
This report examines customer churn trends over time and highlights the key drivers behind customer attrition. It explores churn across segments such as contract type, tenure, and usage behavior to support informed retention and business decisions.

​📡 Telecom Customer Churn Analysis
​Business Intelligence Solution with Power BI

​📝 Project Overview
​This project focuses on Customer Retention within the telecommunications industry. Using a dataset of over 7,000 customers, I built an interactive dashboard to identify why customers are leaving (Churning) and which segments are at the highest risk.

​💡 Key Business Insights
​Contract Risk: Customers on Month-to-Month contracts have a significantly higher churn rate (approx. 23%) compared to those on one or two-year contracts.
​Service Impact: Fiber optic users show higher churn levels, suggesting potential issues with service stability or pricing in that segment.
